Banana Berry Smoothie

Ingridents for Smoothie

  1. 1 small banana
  2. 6 oz. pineapple juice
  3. 1/2 c. ice
  4. 6 oz. blueberries
  5. 6 oz. raspberries or blackberries
  6. 2 tsp. honey
  7. 1 tsp. grated peeled fresh ginger

What are the five steps to making a Smoothie?

For the smoothest results, add items to the blender in this order so they’ll be well incorporated:

  1. Liquid (milk, juice, water, yogurt)
  2. Soft fruits + veggies
  3. Leafy greens
  4. Dry ingredients (seeds, protein powders)
  5. Ice + other frozen ingredients

Method

In blender, purée banana, pineapple juice, ice, blueberries, raspberries or blackberries, honey, and ginger until smooth.
