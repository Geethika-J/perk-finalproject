Slow cooker seafood ramen

Ingridents

  1. 64 oz broth (seafood, vegetable, or chicken)
  2. 4â€“6 oz ramen
  3. 1 lb seafood
  4. 2 green onions, sliced
  5. 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  6. 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  7. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  8. 1/4 cup kale, chopped
  9. 1/2 lb tomatoes, sliced
  10. 1/4 tsp sesame oil
  11. 1 tsp salt
  12. 1/4 tsp pepper
  13. 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes

Method

Add all ingredients except the seafood, kale and ramen to the slow cooker. Stir to mix well.

Cook on high for 2-3 hours, or low for 4-6 hours.

Add seafood, kale & ramen and cook for an additional 15-30 minutes.
