How This Nutrition Plan Works

Don't sweat it.

This meal plan is both flexible and easy, so you can swap any breakfasts for breakfasts, lunches for lunches, and so on.

Drink as many calorie-free beverages (coffee, tea, water, etc.) as you want and have up to two snacks per day.

You can safely lose up to two pounds per week and keep it up until you reach your weight-loss goal.
