Virgin Canberry Basil Sangria

Ingridents for Beverage

  1. 3 c. cranberry juice
  2. Juice of 1 orange (about 1/2 c.)
  3. 1 (12-oz.) can seltzer
  4. 1 orange, sliced
  5. 1 apple, cored and sliced
  6. 1/3 c. frozen cranberries
  7. 1/4 c. packed basil leaves
  8. Ice

Method

In a large pitcher, combine cranberry juice, orange juice, and seltzer. Add fruit and basil and stir to combine.

Pour over ice to serve.
